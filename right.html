Online Examination System

- By Satyavanth

The online examination portal serves as a centralized platform for seamless assessment experiences. Upon arriving at the homepage, users are greeted with a warm welcome and are presented with essential navigation links to streamline their journey. The portal features a robust user authentication system, offering a secure login interface and a user-friendly registration process. The user dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of upcoming exams, schedules, and quick links to ongoing assessments, creating a personalized and efficient experience. Administrators can effortlessly create exams through an intuitive interface, configuring various parameters such as duration, question types, and difficulty levels. A rich question bank, categorized by subject and difficulty, facilitates easy selection and modification of questions. The exam-taking interface is designed for clarity, featuring a timer, navigation buttons, and question marking options. Diverse question types, including multiple-choice, true/false, short answer, matching, and fill-in-the-blank, ensure a versatile testing environment.
